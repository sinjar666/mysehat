ABOUT MYSEHAT

MYSEHAT is a personal healthcare record management system for mobile devices.

The application shall maintain in an electronic form all such data that might be concerned with the healthcare of the user. The data shall be maintained in a way which shall be portable (compatible) enough so that it can be easily understood by the patient himself and contain ample technical data such that it is useful for the treating doctor.
